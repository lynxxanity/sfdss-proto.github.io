San Francisco De Sales

Junior High School

At San Francisco De Sales, we understand that the junior high school years are a pivotal time in a student's academic journey. Our dedicated team of educators is committed to providing a challenging and comprehensive curriculum that not only prepares students for the academic rigor of high school but also nurtures their social and emotional development.

Academically, our junior high school program emphasizes a well-rounded education. Students delve deeper into core subjects such as language arts, mathematics, science, and social studies, fostering critical thinking and analytical skills. Our curriculum is designed to inspire a love for learning, encouraging students to explore their interests and passions while developing a strong foundation for future academic pursuits.

In addition to a strong academic focus, San Francisco De Sales Junior High School offers a variety of extracurricular activities, including clubs, sports, and leadership opportunities. These activities provide students with the chance to discover and cultivate their talents, build teamwork and leadership skills, and create lasting friendships. We believe that a holistic approach to education is key to developing well-rounded individuals who are prepared for success in all aspects of life.

Character development remains a cornerstone of our junior high school program. We are dedicated to instilling values such as integrity, responsibility, and resilience in our students. Our supportive and inclusive community fosters a sense of belonging and encourages students to embrace diversity, empathy, and a spirit of service.

San Francisco De Sales recognizes the importance of preparing students for the challenges of the future. Our junior high school program incorporates technology, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills to ensure that students are not only academically competent but also well-equipped to navigate an ever-evolving world.

Parental involvement is highly valued at San Francisco De Sales, and we actively encourage collaboration between parents, teachers, and students. Regular communication, parent-teacher conferences, and workshops create a strong partnership that supports each student's academic and personal growth.

Join us on this transformative educational experience where every student is empowered to reach their full potential and emerge as confident, compassionate, and capable individuals.
