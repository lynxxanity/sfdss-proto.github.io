San Francisco De Sales

Kindergarten

Welcome to the vibrant and nurturing Kindergarten program at San Francisco De Sales School, where we believe in laying the foundation for a lifetime of learning with joy and discovery. Our dedicated team of educators is committed to providing a warm and engaging environment that fosters curiosity, creativity, and a love for learning.

At San Francisco De Sales School, we recognize the unique developmental needs of young learners. Our Kindergarten program is designed to cultivate a strong sense of community, encouraging each child to explore, question, and grow both academically and socially. We strive to create a safe and supportive atmosphere where children feel secure to express themselves and take on new challenges.

Our curriculum is thoughtfully crafted to integrate academic excellence with hands-on experiences. Through a blend of interactive lessons, play-based activities, and collaborative projects, we aim to instill a love of learning that extends beyond the classroom. Our skilled teachers are dedicated to fostering a love for reading, mathematics, and exploration, ensuring that each child develops a solid foundation for future academic success.

Beyond the classroom, San Francisco De Sales School offers a range of enriching extracurricular activities and events that complement the Kindergarten experience. From special assemblies to field trips and community projects, we believe in providing a well-rounded education that extends beyond textbooks and traditional lessons.

Our commitment to the development of the whole child goes hand in hand with our focus on character formation. We emphasize the importance of values such as respect, responsibility, and kindness, nurturing not only academic skills but also social and emotional intelligence.

As parents, you are integral partners in your child's educational journey. We encourage open communication and collaboration between home and school, fostering a strong support system for each child's growth and development.

Join us at San Francisco De Sales School Kindergarten, where every child is valued, and every day is an opportunity for discovery and growth. We invite you to be a part of our nurturing community that shapes young minds and prepares them for a bright future.
