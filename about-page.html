About our Campus



Welcome to San Francisco De Sales School, where education meets excellence. Established in 1980, we have been a cornerstone of the San Francisco community for several years, providing a nurturing environment that fosters both academic achievement and personal growth. Our dedicated faculty and staff are committed to cultivating a love of learning in every student, from our youngest learners in preschool to our accomplished high school seniors.

At San Francisco De Sales, we believe in holistic education that goes beyond textbooks and classrooms. Our curriculum is designed to not only impart knowledge but also to instill values of empathy, integrity, and respect. We take pride in our vibrant community that encourages collaboration, creativity, and critical thinking. Our graduates go on to excel in various fields, equipped with the skills and character necessary to make a positive impact in the world. Join us on this educational journey, where every student is empowered to reach their fullest potential.
