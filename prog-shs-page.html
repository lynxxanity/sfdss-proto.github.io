San Francisco De Sales

Senior High School

Our senior high school experience is designed to be a transformative journey, preparing students for higher education and empowering them to make informed decisions about their future careers.

STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics): In our STEM program, students engage in a rigorous and hands-on curriculum that delves into the fascinating realms of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. From conducting experiments in state-of-the-art laboratories to exploring the principles of coding and innovation, our STEM program is designed to cultivate critical thinking and problem-solving skills. Students are prepared for the challenges of the ever-evolving technological landscape, laying the foundation for future careers in STEM-related fields.

HUMSS (Humanities and Social Sciences): For those with a passion for understanding the complexities of human society and culture, our HUMSS program offers a rich and comprehensive exploration of the humanities and social sciences. From literature and philosophy to sociology and political science, students in the HUMSS track engage in thought-provoking discussions, hone their analytical skills, and gain a deep understanding of the world around them. This program prepares students for a wide array of careers in fields such as law, education, journalism, and social services.

ABM (Accountancy, Business, and Management): Our ABM program is tailored for students aspiring to become future leaders in the world of business and finance. The curriculum covers essential principles of accountancy, economics, entrepreneurship, and management. Students gain practical skills in financial analysis, business planning, and strategic decision-making. The ABM program not only prepares students for careers in business but also equips them with the knowledge and acumen needed to navigate the complexities of the global economy.

GAS (General Academic Strand): The GAS program provides students with flexibility and a well-rounded education that allows them to explore various fields of study. This versatile program is designed to develop critical thinking, research, and communication skills, preparing students for diverse career paths and higher education opportunities. Students in the GAS track have the freedom to tailor their academic journey, making it an ideal choice for those who wish to explore a range of subjects before making more specialized career decisions.

At San Francisco De Sales, our Senior High School Program is not just about academics; it's about holistic development. Beyond the classroom, students have access to a variety of extracurricular activities, leadership opportunities, and career guidance that complement their academic pursuits. We strive to empower our students to excel academically, embrace their passions, and emerge as well-rounded individuals ready to make a positive impact in their chosen fields. Join us on this exciting educational journey where every student's potential is nurtured, and aspirations are turned into achievements.
